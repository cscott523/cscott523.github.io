Some crafts might take time, others you could do in a minute, either way you still get to have fun and create memories with your family while doing some of these crafts that I am fixing to tell you about.



Different Crafts

  1. Countdown to Christmas: Decorate your tree one day at a time with a calender that decides which ornament to hang up on your tree.
  2. Gift Toppers: Find things around the house like pom poms and use them to make Christmas gift toppers.
  3. Button Tree Ornament: Cut out a Christmas tree color it green then put colorful buttons on it to make it look like a christmas tree.
