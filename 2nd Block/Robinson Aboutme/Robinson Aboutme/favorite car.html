This page is going to be about the Nissan Gtr sports car. The gtr is a hand built 2 door sports car revealed in 2007. Even though the gtr is a soprts car it is one of the best because of its all wheel drive feature. The stock Nissan gtr is capable of going 0 to 60 mile per hour in 2.9 seconds.
The Gtr was first created in Febuary of 1969 at a tokyo motor show it was introduced as a 4 door sports car it was called the nissan Skyline gtr, the second generation was made shortly after this. After a 16 year break they finally created a 3rd generation of the gtr they created it becasue the company found interest in group A racing. More generatins of the car were created but in 2007 is when the car really took off with performance with a 0.27 of drag and a twin turbo engine and 588 nm of torque, this all heled redefine the launch control system forever. since then there hasnt been a new generation released but rather some slight changes done too the car these incude a new grille, headlights, vented hood, chin spoiler, side skirts, and a new rear bumper.
