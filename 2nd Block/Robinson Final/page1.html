What makes Christmas important

Christmas is important because it is a major religious holiday widely celebrated across the world it marks God becoming man in order to save mankind from its sin through the birth of Jesus Christ.

More About Christmas

Although not everyone celebrates christmas it is the largest known holiday around the world. To some people Christmas is about being with family and appreciating what you have.
