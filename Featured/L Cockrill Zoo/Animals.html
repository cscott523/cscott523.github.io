Panda's

Panda's are native to South China, they require much room and atiquite tempetures. The favorite food is bamboo, they also like to sleep in tall trees. Panda's like to be keep in numbers.

Dairy Cow

Dairy Cow's are an exotic animal found in Egypt. They are very rare in America due to the lack of their food source. They are very agressive animals and should be in strong cages

Elephant

Elephant's are very large animals that are found in South Africa. They are very inteligent and have been known to travel to the same spots throughout their life

Giraffe

Giraffe's are very tall Herbivore found in Africa, they are often found in groups. They use their tall necks to eat leaves from the tall tree's

Tiger
