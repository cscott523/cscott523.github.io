Introduction to the Three Main Disney Parks

Disney's Magic Kingdom was the first theme park opened by Disney.
It was opened on October 1, 1971.

Disney's Hollywood Studios was opened in May 1, 1989.
They were competing against rival Universal Studios.

Disney's Animal Kingdom opened on April 22, 1998.
This happened to be Earth Day!
It is currently the fourth most-visited theme park in America.
