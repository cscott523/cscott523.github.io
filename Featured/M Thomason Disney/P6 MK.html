Disney's Magic Kingdom is divided into 7 sections.
These sections each have their own theme and themed rides to match!

  • Adventureland
  • Tomorrowland
  • Frontierland
  • Liberty Square
  • Mickey's Toontown Fair
  • Fantasyland
