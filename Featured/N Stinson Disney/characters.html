Theme Park Rides

Top Theme Park Rides

1. Tomorrowland Transit Authority People Mover




On this ride, you will take a little trip around the second floor of the many attractions of tomorrowland! While on this ride you will take a tour around the park and see all the sights of tomorrowland! This ride continues to be a favored by people who visit Disneyworld!

2. Sorin'



On this ride, you will take a high flying adventure through the world! But how? You will be "sorin'" through the sky getting a birds eye view of the world! You will see places like San francisco and the HUGE bridge that is there as well! No doubt this is a ride worth waiting in line for!

3. The Haunted Mansion



On this ride, you will take a trip through a place that is plagued by gools and ghosts beyond your wildest dreams! This ride will take you on a ride through the famous haunted mansion and show you the countless rooms filled with spookes beyond your wildest dreams! You can be sure that when you get on this ride, you will want to keep going back for the same fun filled feights that the Haunted Mansion has to offer!

4. The Rockin' Rollercoaster



On this ride, you have personally been invited by areosmith to there concert! But how are you going to get there in time? You will be taking the limo that has been called just to get you there in time! You will be shot from 0-60mph in a matter of seconds and then will be tossed and turned to the beat of an areosmith tune! This ride is loved by many and is sure to be loved by you!
