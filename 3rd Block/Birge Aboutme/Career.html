Cloud Architect


Education
You need a bachelor's degree in computer science, softwate engineering, or other related program. Some advanced engineer positions will require a master's degree in business administration (MBA)

What do they do?
A cloud architect is an IT professional who is responsible for over seeing a company's cloud computing strategy. This includes cloud adobtion plans,cloud aplication design, cloud management, and cloud monitering.

Salary
A Junior Solutions Cloud Architect makes around $105,000 /yr. and a Senior Solutions Cloud Architect makes around 375,000 /yr.
