A Software Appliations Devolpers job is to analyze the needs of consumers and create software and applications that will complement the consumers needs. The software and applications that are created are then sold to the consumer for commercial use.



It is important to understand CS fundamentals for this career, this can be learned through school or apprenticeship. Also a previous background is recomended, somone with just an education without any experince is less interesting.



In 2014, the median salary for this career was $95,510. The highest 10% in this profession earned $149,480, the lowest paid only earned $56,310.
