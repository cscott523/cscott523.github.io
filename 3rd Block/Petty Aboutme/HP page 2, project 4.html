HUMMER

Alpha H1

The Hummer H1 Alpha was a one-year production model of the regular H1 that featured a handful of important mechanical changes. A more powerful engine, another gear for the transmission and bigger brakes greatly improved performance over the non-Alpha H1 and made the beast the best it had ever been.

Luxury H3

Then came the Hummer H3. It was designed to be the "real world" Hummer: less pyrotechnics and more sparklers. he H3's styling still proclaimed loudly that it's a card-carrying member of the Hummer guild, and the truck had more off-road capability than just about every other midsize SUV when the pavement ends.

Off-Road H3

The Hummer H2 debuted in 2003 as an attempt by Hummer and parent company General Motors to offer something a bit more practical than the exotic, designed-for-military-use H1.When examined solely on these merits, the H2 seemed like the ultimate pick for an SUV.
