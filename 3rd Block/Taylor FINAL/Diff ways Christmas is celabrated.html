Hanukkah isn't just one day it's actually eight. And can also be called Chanukah. Kwanzaa us derived from the phrase "matunda ya kwanza" wihch means "first fruits" in Swahili. Kwanzaa is celabrated differently by family. But celebrations often include certain songs and different dances. On each of the seven nights, the family gathers together and a child from the family and lights one of the candles on the Kinara or candleholder (seen below). Then one of the seven principals are conversed. The Seven principles are called Nguzo Saba(The Seven Principles in Swhili).
