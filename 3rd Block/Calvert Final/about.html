About Santas Workshop

Santas Workshop was founded in 1963 by William Enrock. Enrock gave the company to his son, Leroy Enrok in the late 1980's. Enrock has since then created a national online christmas store, receiving various awards since the early 2000's. "I didn't think we would have come this far. I don't know a lot about marketing, but I have always had a feel for christmas items." sai Enrock.
