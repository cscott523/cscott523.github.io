Christmas ornament may have had a humble beginnings. In the early 1800s, fruits (especially apples) and nuts are the first Christmas ornaments used to decorate the Christmas tree. Among German families (who popularized the Christmas tree) they make Christmas ornament from carrots and other hard cookies baked in the shape of fruits, stars, bells, angels and hearts.

In other countries, such as America, many of the first Christmas ornament were long strands of cranberries and popcorn to circle their trees! When you think about Christmas ornament, most people initially think of Christmas baubles as the most common ornament. Christmas ball is a timeless symbol of the tree, and possibly the best-selling Christmas ornaments ever. There are three main types of Christmas balls ornaments – glass, plastic and other materials.

The trend of using Christmas ornaments for decoration came into vogue since long. Shining glass reflector ornaments, shiny Christmas balls and shining stars, hanging from trees make the beauty and charm of the joy of the holiday.
