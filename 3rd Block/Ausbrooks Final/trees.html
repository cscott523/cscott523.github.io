Trees








Even though most people know these trees as "christmas trees" and that is
usually what they are called, these trees are actually named the "evergreen
fir tree". They have usually been used to celebrate winter festivals for thousands
of years. Some people had used branches of it to decorate their homes during
the winter season. The reason behing this is it made them think of the upcoming
spring season. Others used Fir Trees to decorate their temples at the festival
of Saturnalia. Christians use it as a sign of everlasting life with God.
